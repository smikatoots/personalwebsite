Diaspora Project

I did a mini data visualization project that explored the patterns of Philippine immigration through the years. I'm really passionate about Filipinos all over the world, and I wanted to trace out the numbers of Filipino diaspora members globally! I used (sparse) data from phil.gov. I used R for analysis and preliminary visualization and Excel for extensive visualization.

Research Projects

    I try a range data-related projects in fields such as economics, psychology, bioinformatics, etc. If you'd like to hear more about the projects, feel free to ask. Here are some examples:

  • Cross-Country Analysis of Gender Wage Gaps and GDP: relationship between GDP growth and wage gap inequality and how various other factors weigh into this relationship.
  • Playing the Race Card: examined the perception that when Whites claim bias, Blacks are at a higher advantage than Whites are when Blacks claim.
  • Parent-Child Relationships and Grades Among Adolescents: examined associations with psychological factors that affect grades
  • Effect of Coconut Oil on the Peroxisomal Beta Oxidation of Fatty Acids: does coconut oil can significantly decrease or increase the rate of inhibition of peroxisomal beta-oxidation?
  • Comparative Analysis of Sequence and Structur of DENV and CHIKV Virus: compared structures of the four DENV serotypes with CHIKV to provide information for further studies on treatments.

about

background education leadership fun facts
I did not discover my love for technology like other 14-year olds who tore apart their computers and saw how cool they were. I discovered it as an 18-year old when I moved 8,207 miles away from my home and needed a means to connect with the place I missed most; when I realized the need for more innovation around financial solutions to grow small businesses in third-world economies; when, during my design obsession, I wanted an immediate way to learn how many post-its it took to circle the world (508,000,00 btw.)

I discovered it when I saw its potential to solve human problems, influence behaviors through good (or bad!) design, and connect millions from seemingly disparate worlds, all with a click of a button. I thrive at the core of tech x design x business. My biggest dream is to improve the growing tech and startup scenes in third-world economies, starting with my home, the Philippines.

My philosophies:
  • Cross-disciplinary thinking - we need a variety of perspectives in problem solving.
  • People-centric, behavior-driven - we design for our human users, not for selfish needs.
  • Crave feedback - prototype, iterate, repeat. Failure is crucial to success.
  • Data-driven - insights are drawn from asking the right questions and crafting the best stories.
  • Empathy is core - towards users and people under your leadership. No one does it alone.
  • Self-learning - Google is my best friend.
Skills: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Redux, SQL, STATA, Python, Excel & VBA, web Analytics, Photoshop, Sketch, digital marketing, human-centered design, product design, UX/UI design, startups, storytelling
